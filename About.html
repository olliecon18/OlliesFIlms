About Me

Who am I?

Hi there, my name is Ollie Conwell, I am 21 years old, currently studying a Bachelor of Media and Communication Course.

Ever since I can remember I have always shown a passion for cinema, so much so that I even worked at one for 4 years.

Watching films allow me to escape, relax, laugh, and motivate me. I have created this website as a personal blog, where I upload a variety of different reviews and recommendations. I hope you enjoy and find a film to watch!

Some of my favourite films: The Shining, Blade Runner 2049, Akira, Back to the Future, Prisoners, 2001: A Space Odyssey. Read more about these in the 'Ollies Favourites' section at the top of the page.

Some of my favourite directors: Denis Villeneuve, Stanley Kubrick, Ari Aster, Quentin Tarantino, Christopher Nolan.

Some of my favourite genre's: Thriller, Sci-Fi, Animation, Comedy.
