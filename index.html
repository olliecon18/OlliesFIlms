Ollies Recommendations of the Month

Featured below are 3 films carefuly chosen monthly in which I have seen and recommend.

Rush Hour

Considered one of the funniest buddy-cop films of the 90's, Rush Hour is a classic comedy that still is a hilarious watch for all ages.

Hereditary

One of the few films that cemented itself into my brain for days after watching. Hereditary is a rollercoaster experience backed by an amazing cast and soundtrack. This film is one of my favourite horror films of all time.

The Truman Show

One of Jim Carrey's most recognised films, The Truman Show is a classic that should be watched amongst all cinephile's.
